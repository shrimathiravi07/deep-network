The Digital Constellation

Imagine a smart city where every citizen’s voice floats like a digital message in the clouds. A citizen spots an encroachment in a lake and fires a complaint into the system—location tagged, photo attached. In the control tower, officers see a glowing dashboard: pending alerts orbit around. When an officer takes action, they upload proof—an image of the restored water body. The complaint transforms: a red orbit turns green, signaling successfully actioned. If no action is taken, the orbit stays suspended, hovering as pending, waiting for justice. "Every click, upload, and action becomes part of a transparent, auditable digital constellation guiding the city to water protection."
